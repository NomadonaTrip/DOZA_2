What we do

Our mission goes beyond the four walls of the church—we are called to impact the world. DOZMI actively engages in various mission outreaches to spread the love of Christ, disciple nations, and meet both spiritual and physical needs.

Bible Schools

Raising a new generation of leaders equipped with sound doctrine and kingdom principles.

Crusades and Evangelism

Boldly preaching the gospel, demonstrating God’s power, and bringing deliverance to communities.

Seminars and Conferences

Empowering believers through faith-building teachings and prophetic impartations.

Church Planting

Establishing vibrant church communities that disciple and transform cities.

Feeding & Clothing Programs (Africa & North America)

Providing food, clothing, and hope to those in need.

Mothers' prayer summit

Interceding for children and families, raising a generation rooted in God’s promises.

Outreach to the homeless (Canada)

In partnership with Relentless Worship Ministries, we reach out with love, care packages, and the gospel.

What we believe

Our mission goes beyond the four walls of the church—we are called to impact the world. DOZMI actively engages in various mission outreaches to spread the love of Christ, disciple nations, and meet both spiritual and physical needs.

We believe in the death, resurrection, and Lordship of Jesus Christ.

We believe in salvation through grace and faith in Jesus.

We believe in water baptism and the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues.

We believe in the power of prayer, communion, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the supremacy of God’s Word as the final authority in all matters of faith and practice.
