Our Ministries

God has called us to serve Him through various ministries that touch lives and build community. Discover where you can get involved and make a difference.

Kids Church

A fun, safe, and nurturing environment where children ages 3-12 can learn about God's love through engaging activities, Bible stories, and age-appropriate worship. Our dedicated team creates memorable experiences that help children grow in their faith journey.

Lifegroups

Connect, grow, and do life together in our small group communities. Lifegroups provide an intimate setting for fellowship, Bible study, prayer, and mutual support. Experience authentic relationships and spiritual growth in a welcoming environment.

Prayer Ministry

Join our dedicated prayer warriors as we intercede for our church, community, and world. Through corporate prayer meetings, prayer chains, and individual intercession, we seek God's face and see His power at work in mighty ways.
